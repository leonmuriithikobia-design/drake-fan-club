Drake — Life, Music & Legacy

Who Is Drake?

Aubrey Drake Graham, known professionally as **Drake**, is a Canadian rap and music superstar — one of the most influential artists of the 21st century. He first rose to fame as a teen actor on *Degrassi: The Next Generation* before becoming one of the best-selling music artists worldwide. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Ontario, Drake began his music career with early mixtapes and later signed with Lil Wayne’s Young Money Entertainment in 2009. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Early Life & Rise

Drake was born into a musically connected family — his father is a drummer — and his first major public role was acting before he switched focus completely to music. After releasing several early mixtapes like *So Far Gone*, he achieved major success and became an international music icon. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Drake also founded the record label **OVO Sound** and has been involved in business ventures including fashion and partnerships in entertainment. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Achievements & Legacy

Drake is one of the world’s best-selling music artists, selling over 170 million records. He’s won multiple awards including Grammys, Billboard Music Awards, and more — and he holds records on the Billboard charts for most entries and longest chart presence. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Through his music and business work, Drake helped define modern hip‑hop and R&B, blending genres and bringing emotional expression into mainstream rap. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
