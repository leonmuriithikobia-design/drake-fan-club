About Drake

:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}, born Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, and songwriter. He first gained recognition as an actor on the TV show Degrassi before transitioning into music.

Rising to fame in the late 2000s, Drake became one of the most influential artists in hip-hop and R&B. Known for blending rap with singing, he has released multiple chart-topping albums and hit songs like “God’s Plan,” “One Dance,” and “Hotline Bling.”

His unique style, emotional lyrics, and global impact have made him one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
